Each participant of the presale gains access to the page presale.getlambo.xyz. On the presale page, you can:

enter the amount of ETH you want to participate in the presale with.


choose the level of the profit that you will receive when the token price increases.

* We do not guarantee the token growth, but presale algorithm reduces the possibility of dumping by early participants.

Once you've selected the settings, 20% of your ETH will go to a special V3 pool on Uniswap with matching settings. The smart contract will automatically calculate and close your position when the L2M token's market price reaches the desired profit. Presale participants won't directly get L2M tokens, but they can influence the initial settings of the presale smart contract and their own position. The rest of the ETH will provide initial liquidity for the main L2M token pool on Uniswap V2, and it will be locked for at least 1 year from the project's launch.


Example. if your deposit is 10 ETH and choose X10 profit, the smart contract send 8 ETH to V2 and marekting. And will open a 2 ETH position. When the market price of the token reaches the exit price, the smart contract will close the position and send ETH to your wallet (this example, 100 ETH).

After transferring ETH to the presale pool, you'll be able to track your position on a dedicated page. The page will display the current token price, the number of tokens in your position, and the closing point where you'll receive the ETH.
